HOW TO MOUNT: 
    Drive must be exFAT for simplicity

    Identify drive(s): lsblk

    > Create a mount point: sudo mkdir /media/$DRIVENAME
        > Mount drive to mount point: sudo mount /dev/SDA2* /media/media01 EXAMPLE**
    > Mount on startup:
        > Get UUID of drive: sudo blkid
            > Open fstab: sudo nano /etc/fstab
                > Add new line: UUID=your-drive-uuid /media/media01 exFAT defaults 0 2
    > Permissions: sudo chmod -R 755 /media/media01
                    sudo chmod -R 666 /media/media01
